Drone Race

•Contestants can form a team of 4 members.
• The event has 1 round.
A starting position and final position along with the track will be given. Various obstacles will be placed in the track and the one who reaches with less time and without touching the obstacle wins the game.

Tech Senate

• Participation is expected from a group of 4 members.

• The group will be given a chance to present their bill (idea/project) infront of the parliament (15 min).

• Then the contestants are allowed to express their opinions about the bills presented and answer the questions raised by others.


• Each member of the parliament is given a vote, the bill with maximum number of votes is chosen as the best bill.

Prize worth: 8,000/-

Soccer Race

• Participants will have to build their wheel robots(wired) for playing the soccer match using the components provided at the beginning of the event.
• Components for making the bots will be given according to the virtual bidding system and design details will also be given at the begning of the event.
• One ball will be placed at the center of arena and robots are initially placed at their respective goal posts.
• Time limit is 5 minutes.
• The robot which gain maximum goals will be the winner.
• The team can have 2 to 3 members.

Prize worth: 8,000/-
